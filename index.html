Questions

Common lawn care questions.

Clear answers reduce hesitation and make it easier for visitors to request a quote without needing to call first.

Do you offer one-time mowing?

Yes. One-time cuts are available for move-ins, parties, listing photos, vacations, or yards that simply need a reset.

How much does lawn service cost?

Most basic visits start around $45, but the final quote depends on yard size, grass height, trimming, access, and service frequency.

Can I request weekly or biweekly service?

Yes. Weekly service is best during heavy growing season, while biweekly service can work for smaller or slower-growing yards.

What should I include in my quote request?

Include your address or neighborhood, the service needed, photos if available, whether there is a fenced backyard, and your preferred schedule.
