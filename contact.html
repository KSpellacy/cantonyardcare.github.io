Quote checklist

Ask for what a lawn care business needs to respond quickly.

  • Name: Who should reply?
  • Neighborhood: Where is the property located?
  • Service needed: Mowing, cleanup, mulch, trimming, or other.
  • Yard size: Small, medium, large, or unknown.
  • Timeline: One-time, weekly, biweekly, or ASAP.
  • Photos: Can they send photos for a better quote?
